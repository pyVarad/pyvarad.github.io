# 📘 **Getting Started with System Design**

This document summarizes the fundamental concepts and guidelines to kickstart your learning in system design. It is based on handwritten notes covering key principles, sample questions, and essential knowledge areas for interviews.

---

## 1️⃣ **Design Fundamentals**

To succeed in system design interviews, you need to focus on three foundational areas:

1. **Coding Skills**
2. **Problem-Solving Ability**
3. **Understanding Data Structures**

---

## 2️⃣ **Sample Interview Questions**

Expect questions that test your ability to design scalable systems. For example:

1. Design YouTube.
2. Design a system with a million users.

---

## 3️⃣ **Interview Process Breakdown**

- **Coding Interview:**  
  *Prerequisite:* Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA)

- **System Design Interview:**  
  *Prerequisite:* Strong grasp of **Design Fundamentals**

---

## 4️⃣ **What Are Design Fundamentals?**

System design interviews typically include challenges such as:

- Design YouTube
- Design Uber

👉 **Key Insight:**  
It is the *responsibility of the interviewee* to ask clarifying questions and make the discussion interactive.  
➡️ **This requires solid knowledge of design fundamentals.**

---

## 5️⃣ **Coding Interview vs. System Design Interview**

| Coding Interview                          | System Design Interview                      |
|------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Objective (works/doesn’t work)            | Subjective (justify your solution)           |
| Focus on correctness & optimization      | Focus on explaining reasoning & trade-offs   |

**Example for coding:**  
- Does the program work?
- Is it optimal or non-optimal?

**Example for system design:**  
- Justify your solution.
- Explain the choices made and the reasoning behind them.
